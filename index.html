About me

Full stack web developer with five years of biological science experience and a Bachelor's degree with major emphasis on teamwork and communication. Projects and experiments conducted range from organic/inorganic chemical analysis, to forensics, and microbiological gene editing, all with emphasis on meticulous note-taking, standard operating procedure, and creative team brainstorming. I have always been tech minded and mesh very well with the aesthetic web programming languages of CSS libraries as well as the logical components like JavaScript. Excited to be working with the MERN stack. Half of all science is articulating thoughts with others and bouncing ideas off each other which is my favorite way to learn and listen.
